Refrigerator Repair

Top 5 Most Common Refrigerator Issues We Fix in Carmel Homes

A refrigerator is one of the hardest-working appliances in your home, running 24/7 to keep food fresh and safe. However, when it starts overworking, it can lead to higher energy bills, reduced efficiency, and potential mechanical failure. Knowing the signs of an overworking refrigerator and addressing them promptly can save you from costly repairs or premature replacement. 


If you're searching for refrigerator repair services near me, this blog will guide you through the top five most common refrigerator issues we encounter and how our professional team resolves them."

  1. Refrigerator Not Cooling Properly

One of the most concerning refrigerator problems is when the appliance isn’t cooling properly. This issue can cause your food to spoil, leading to unnecessary waste and potential health risks. Several factors could be at play:

Common Causes:

  • Faulty Thermostat or Temperature Sensor: If the thermostat isn’t working properly, the fridge won’t maintain the correct internal temperature.
  • Dirty or Blocked Condenser Coils: Over time, dust and debris can accumulate on the condenser coils, making it harder for the refrigerator to cool down.
  • Compressor Issues: The compressor is responsible for circulating refrigerant throughout the system, and a malfunction can lead to inadequate cooling.
  • Broken Evaporator Fan: If the evaporator fan isn’t working, cool air won’t circulate properly inside the fridge.

What We Do:

At Alex Repair, our refrigerator repair Carmel specialists begin by thoroughly inspecting the appliance. We check the thermostat settings and temperature sensors for accuracy, clean any debris from the condenser coils, and test the evaporator fan for proper function. If the compressor is malfunctioning, we can either repair or replace it with high-quality parts to ensure long-term reliability. If you're facing issues with cooling, reach out to our team, and we'll have your fridge working at optimal temperatures in no time.

  1. Excessive Frost Build-Up in the Freezer

Frost buildup in the freezer compartment is another common issue that homeowners in Carmel face. While some frost is normal, excessive ice accumulation can lead to reduced storage space, poor performance, and unnecessary energy consumption.

Common Causes:

  • Defrost Timer or Heater Failure: If the defrost timer or heater is broken, ice will accumulate inside the freezer instead of melting.
  • Damaged Door Seals: Worn-out or broken door gaskets can let warm air inside, which can cause frost to form.
  • Blocked Air Vents: Air circulation is vital for maintaining an even temperature. Blocked vents can prevent cold air from circulating, leading to ice buildup.
  • Faulty Thermostat: A malfunctioning thermostat can cause the temperature inside the freezer to drop too low, causing excessive frost formation.

What We Do:

Our technicians are trained to identify the root cause of frost buildup in your freezer. We begin by defrosting the unit and checking the defrost timer and heater. If they are faulty, we’ll replace them. We also inspect the door seals for any air leaks and ensure proper ventilation by clearing blocked vents. By addressing both the mechanical and environmental factors that lead to frost buildup, we can restore your freezer’s efficiency and save you money on energy bills.

  1. Water Leaking on the Floor

Water pooling around the base of your refrigerator can be a major inconvenience and potentially lead to water damage or mold growth in your kitchen. This issue can often be traced to:

Common Causes:

  • Clogged or Frozen Defrost Drain: The defrost drain helps direct excess water to a drainage pan beneath the fridge. If it’s blocked or frozen, water will pool inside the fridge or leak onto the floor.
  • Faulty Water Supply Line: If your refrigerator has a water dispenser or ice maker, a damaged or loose water supply line can result in leaks.
  • Broken Water Inlet Valve: The water inlet valve controls the flow of water into your fridge’s ice maker and water dispenser. If it’s broken, it can cause leaks.
  • Damaged Ice Maker: Sometimes, a malfunctioning ice maker can cause water to leak out of the appliance.

What We Do:

When we perform a refrigerator repair in Carmel, we start by inspecting the defrost drain and clearing any blockages or frozen sections. Our technicians also check the water supply lines for cracks, leaks, or disconnections. If necessary, we replace the water inlet valve or ice maker components. Our goal is to address the underlying problem and ensure your refrigerator is leak-free, saving you from costly water damage repairs.

  1. Unusual Noises or Vibrations

If your refrigerator starts making strange noises, it’s usually a sign that something isn’t right. From buzzing and humming to clicking or vibrating, different sounds indicate different issues. The most common causes of unusual refrigerator noises include:

Common Causes:

  • Compressor or Fan Problems: A malfunctioning compressor or condenser fan motor can produce loud humming or buzzing noises.
  • Vibrating or Loose Parts: Over time, parts can become loose, causing vibrations when the appliance is running.
  • Worn-out Evaporator Fan: If the evaporator fan is damaged, it can cause a clicking sound as it struggles to turn.
  • Improperly Levelled Refrigerator: If your refrigerator isn’t level, it can create vibrations as it operates.

What We Do:

Our team at Alex Repair performs a thorough inspection to identify the source of the noise. We examine the compressor, fans, and motors to see if any parts are worn or malfunctioning. If loose parts are found, we tighten or replace them to eliminate vibrations. We also ensure that your refrigerator is properly levelled to avoid additional noise issues. Once we’ve resolved the problem, your refrigerator will run smoothly and quietly again.

  1. Ice Maker Not Working

Refrigerators with built-in ice makers are incredibly convenient, but when they stop working, it can be a major inconvenience. Some of the most common reasons for ice maker issues include:

Common Causes:

  • Clogged Water Supply Line: Ice makers rely on a steady supply of water. If the water line is blocked or frozen, it can prevent ice production.
  • Faulty Water Inlet Valve: If the water inlet valve isn’t functioning, it will prevent water from reaching the ice maker.
  • Improper Temperature: Ice makers work best when the freezer is at the correct temperature. If it’s too warm, the ice maker may fail to produce ice.
  • Defective Ice Maker Module: Sometimes, the ice maker itself can malfunction, requiring repair or replacement.

What We Do:

When you book a refrigerator repair Carmel service with us, we start by checking the water supply line for clogs or ice blockages. We inspect the water inlet valve to ensure it’s working correctly and test the freezer’s temperature to make sure it’s set at the ideal level for ice production. If necessary, we replace the ice maker module or other faulty components to restore full functionality. Our goal is to get your ice maker working perfectly again so you can enjoy ice on demand.
