Is This What Your Bosch Appliance Is Doing?

These are the most common symptoms Zionsville homeowners describe when calling about their Bosch appliances. If any of these match your situation, call (463) 248-8429 for same-day service:

"Dishwasher won't start and shows E15 on the display"

The E15 error means water has accumulated in the base pan and the Aquastop anti-flood sensor has activated. This is often caused by a minor leak from a hose or door seal — we locate and fix the source, then reset the sensor.

"Dishwasher leaves standing water after every cycle"

Bosch dishwasher drainage failures typically involve either a stuck check valve flap or a filter screen clogged with mineral deposits from hard water. We clear and inspect the entire drain path before replacing any component.

"Oven door hangs lower on one side and doesn't seal"

Bosch oven door hinge failures cause uneven door alignment and heat loss during cooking. We carry hinge assemblies for the most common Bosch oven models and complete this repair without removing the oven from the cavity.
