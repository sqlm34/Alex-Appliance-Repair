Washer Not Starting

Power issues, faulty control panels, or door latch problems can stop your washer. We quickly diagnose and fix the issue.

Leaking Water


Damaged hoses, faulty seals, or clogged drains can cause leaks. We inspect and repair to prevent water damage.

Not Draining Properly

Error Codes on Display

Preventive Maintenance Tips for Your Washer

Regular maintenance keeps your washer running smoothly and prevents costly repairs.

  • Clean the Washer Drum - Residue and mold can build up inside the drum. Run a cleaning cycle with vinegar or a washer cleaner monthly.
  • Check Hoses Regularly - Cracked or loose hoses can cause leaks. Inspect and replace them every few years to prevent water damage.
  • Use the Right Detergent - Too much or the wrong detergent creates excess suds and buildup. Use high-efficiency (HE) detergent as recommended.
  • Schedule Professional Maintenance - A yearly check-up from a local washer repair service helps catch issues early. Preventive care keeps your washer running efficiently.
  • Leave the Door Open After Use - Keeping the door slightly open lets moisture escape. This prevents mold and foul odors inside the washer.
  • Inspect and Clean the Filter - A clogged filter affects drainage and performance. Clean it regularly for efficient washing cycles.
  • Level the Washer - An unbalanced washer vibrates and wears out parts faster. Adjust the legs to keep it stable.
