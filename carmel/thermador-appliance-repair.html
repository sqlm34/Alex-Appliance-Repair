"Thermador star burner clicks but won't ignite"

Thermador's star-shaped burner igniter failures require specific Thermador replacement parts — the star geometry means generic igniters won't fit or perform correctly. We stock authentic Thermador star burner components.

"Thermador oven displays an F error code and won't operate"

Thermador oven F-codes (F1 through F9) indicate specific control board or sensor faults. F1 typically means control board failure; F3 means oven temperature sensor. We diagnose the specific code before recommending any component replacement.

"Thermador induction cooktop shows a zone error"

Thermador Freedom induction zone errors occur when the sensor can't recognize cookware or when the induction coil has developed a fault. Coil testing determines whether the issue is a sensor calibration or a genuine coil failure.
