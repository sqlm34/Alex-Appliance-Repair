Is This What Your Samsung Appliance Is Doing?

These are the most common symptoms Carmel homeowners describe when calling about their Samsung appliances. If any of these match your situation, call (463) 248-8429 for same-day service:

"Refrigerator is running but not cold"

The compressor may be working while the evaporator fan or defrost heater has failed. This is one of the most common Samsung refrigerator complaints and is fully repairable without replacing the unit.

"Washer displays an error code and stops mid-cycle"

Samsung front-loaders frequently throw UE (unbalanced), 4E (water supply), or OE (drain) codes. Most are caused by sensor failures, pump clogs, or door latch issues — all repaired at your home in a single visit.

"Dishwasher leaves dishes wet or dirty"

Samsung dishwashers have known issues with the circulation pump and spray arm clogging over time, especially in hard-water areas. A thorough diagnosis identifies whether it needs a part replacement or a deep cleaning service.
