Is This What Your Sub-Zero Appliance Is Doing?

These are the most common symptoms Carmel homeowners describe when calling about their Sub-Zero appliances. If any of these match your situation, call (463) 248-8429 for same-day service:

"Sub-Zero refrigerator is warmer than usual and running more frequently"

This is almost always caused by condenser coil fouling. Sub-Zero's vacuum condenser collects dust and pet hair at the bottom of the unit, reducing cooling efficiency over time. Annual cleaning prevents this — we provide this service and reset optimal performance in a single visit.

"Ice maker produces very little ice or hollow ice cubes"

Sub-Zero ice maker under-production is usually caused by low water pressure from a partially closed water supply valve, or by a failing water inlet valve that doesn't open fully. Both are correctable without replacing the ice maker assembly.

"Refrigerator or freezer door doesn't seal correctly"

Sub-Zero door gasket deterioration on BI-series and Pro series models is a normal maintenance item after 8–12 years. We source authentic Sub-Zero gaskets and install them with the correct compression for a proper seal.
