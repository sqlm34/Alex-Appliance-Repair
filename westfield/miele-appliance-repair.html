Is This What Your Miele Appliance Is Doing?

These are the most common symptoms Westfield homeowners describe when calling about their Miele appliances. If any of these match your situation, call (463) 248-8429 for same-day service:

"Miele dishwasher shows F11 and won't fill with water"

The F11 error on Miele dishwashers indicates a water intake fault — the inlet valve is not opening or water pressure is insufficient. We test inlet valve operation and verify home water pressure before replacing any component.

"Miele washer drum makes a rumbling noise during spin"

Miele drum bearing wear manifests as a low rumble that intensifies with spin speed. Early bearing service on a Miele washer is significantly less expensive than waiting for the bearing to fully fail and damage the drum shaft.

"Miele condenser dryer has water sitting in the bottom"

Miele heat pump and condenser dryer drainage problems are almost always caused by a blocked condensate pump filter — a maintenance item that most owners don't know exists. We clear the filter and test the pump in a single visit.
