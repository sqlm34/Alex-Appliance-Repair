Is This What Your Maytag Appliance Is Doing?

These are the most common symptoms Noblesville homeowners describe when calling about their Maytag appliances. If any of these match your situation, call (463) 248-8429 for same-day service:

"Washer fills and drains but won't agitate"

Maytag top-load washer agitation failure is most commonly caused by worn agitator dogs — small plastic couplings inside the agitator. This is a relatively inexpensive repair that most homeowners don't realize is possible.

"Dryer makes a loud squeal every time it runs"

Maytag dryer squealing is a drum bearing or drum support roller wearing out. Left unaddressed, the drum can damage the dryer cabinet. We carry the correct bearing kits for many current Maytag dryer models.

"Freezer is full of frost and food is thawing"

Maytag refrigerators with defrost problems accumulate frost on the evaporator coils until airflow is blocked entirely. A defrost heater or thermostat replacement solves this permanently.
