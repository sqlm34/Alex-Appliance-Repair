Is This What Your Viking Appliance Is Doing?

These are the most common symptoms McCordsville homeowners describe when calling about their Viking appliances. If any of these match your situation, call (463) 248-8429 for same-day service:

"Viking oven igniter glows but oven won't light"

A Viking gas oven igniter that glows orange but doesn't ignite the burner is a classic weak igniter symptom. The igniter draws current but not enough to open the safety gas valve. Igniter replacement is the definitive repair.

"Viking refrigerator is warm but makes no unusual noise"

Silent Viking refrigerator failures — where the unit runs quietly but doesn't cool — usually indicate a sealed system issue (low refrigerant) or a failed evaporator fan. A sealed system repair is complex work that requires professional technicians with EPA 608 certification.

"Viking range control panel lights up but buttons don't work"

Viking Professional series control board failures manifest as non-responsive touchpads. Control board replacement restores full oven and range functionality.
