"Washer has a wet smell and water puddles under it"

Electrolux front-load washer door boot seal deterioration is common after 5–7 years and in homes with hard water. The rubber bellows develops cracks that leak and accumulate mildew. Boot seal replacement is a transformative repair that eliminates both the leak and the odor source.

"Dryer shuts off too quickly or won't heat at all"

Electrolux dryer thermistor failures cause either premature shutdown (dryer thinks it's overheating) or no heat at all (thermal cutoff has opened). We test the full heating circuit before replacing any individual component.

"Refrigerator temperature alarm keeps going off"

Electrolux refrigerator temperature alarms on the French door series are often traced to a failing temperature control board or a worn door gasket allowing warm air infiltration. We diagnose both before recommending a repair path.
