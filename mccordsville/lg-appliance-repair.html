Is This What Your LG Appliance Is Doing?

These are the most common symptoms McCordsville homeowners describe when calling about their LG appliances. If any of these match your situation, call (463) 248-8429 for same-day service:

"Refrigerator makes a clicking sound then goes warm"

This is the classic symptom of LG linear compressor relay failure. The relay tries to start the compressor repeatedly, fails, and clicks off. This is a well-documented LG repair that our technicians complete regularly.

"Washer shakes violently during spin cycle"

LG front-loaders develop bearing wear over time that causes severe vibration and noise during the spin cycle. Early-stage bearing service is far less expensive than waiting until the drum becomes damaged.

"Range burners ignite but oven won't heat"

LG ranges commonly develop bake element or igniter failures independent of burner function. A full diagnostic identifies whether the element, igniter, or control board is responsible.
