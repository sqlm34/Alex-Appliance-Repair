Dishwasher Repair Services Leak and cleaning issues fixed

Let's be real: a dishwasher is supposed to make your life easier. It should handle everyday plates, cooking pots, and the pile of dishes after dinner without you hand-washing everything.

Then it stops working, and suddenly the appliance that was supposed to help is making your kitchen worse. Maybe dishes come out dirty, maybe water is leaking onto the floor, or maybe the dishwasher will not start at all. Alex Appliance Repair handles these problems every week, and most of them are fixable without replacing the whole unit.

The Dishwasher That Cleans Nothing

This is one of the most frustrating dishwasher problems because it looks like the machine is working. It runs, water flows, and the cycle finishes. Then you open the door and find food residue, cloudy glasses, and pans that look like they were never washed.

Usually the cause is clogged spray arms, a blocked filter, low water pressure, or a weak wash pump. Sometimes the loading pattern blocks water from reaching dishes. When it is actually the machine, these issues can usually be diagnosed and repaired.

Water Leaking Becomes Your Problem Too

A leaking dishwasher gets more expensive the longer it is ignored. A small puddle can spread into cabinetry, flooring, and subfloor damage. Common causes include a worn door seal, faulty inlet valve, drain pump issue, loose hose, or cracked tub.

The sooner you address dishwasher leaks, the easier and cheaper the repair usually is.

It Won't Start At All

If you press the button and nothing happens, the issue may be electrical, a tripped breaker, a failed door latch, a bad control board, or a broken start switch. Some dishwashers will not run unless the door is properly latched, so a small part can make the whole unit look dead.

The Odor Problem Nobody Wants to Discuss

If your dishwasher works but smells bad, the cause is often bacterial growth in the filter, pump area, or drain path. Many homeowners do not realize their dishwasher has a filter that needs regular cleaning. Professional cleaning may be needed when odor keeps coming back.

What Actually Happens When You Call

  1. We listen to the symptom. Is it not cleaning, leaking, not starting, making noise, or smelling bad?
  2. We run and observe a cycle. We watch water flow, listen for abnormal sounds, and look for leaks.
  3. We inspect key parts. We check seals, spray arms, filters, pumps, hoses, latches, and controls.
  4. We test components when needed. We diagnose instead of guessing.
  5. We explain the repair clearly. You get the problem, the price, and the repair options before work begins.

Dishwasher Brands We Work On

We repair Bosch, Miele, KitchenAid, Whirlpool, LG, Samsung, GE, Electrolux, Maytag, Kenmore, and other dishwasher brands. European brands like Bosch and Miele have different designs than many American models, and experience with those differences matters.

The Repair Decision

A dishwasher less than 8 years old is usually worth repairing when it needs a straightforward part replacement. A dishwasher that is 12 years old or older and needs several expensive parts may be better to replace. We will not push repair when replacement makes more sense, and we will not suggest replacement when a practical repair will solve the problem.

Common Scenarios We Handle

We see clogged filters that stop water circulation, worn door seals that cause leaks, control boards that make a good dishwasher appear dead, and persistent odor caused by buildup in the pump assembly. In many cases, the repair is much simpler than the customer expected.

Maintenance You Actually Should Do

  • Clean the dishwasher filter monthly.
  • Use rinse aid to help drying and reduce film on glasses.
  • Run dishwasher cleaner periodically to prevent odor and buildup.
  • Do not overstuff the racks because water needs room to spray.
  • Check spray arms for food particles or clogged openings.

When You Should Call

If your dishes are not getting clean, water is pooling under the dishwasher, the unit will not start, the drain is not working, the machine is making unusual noises, or there is a persistent smell, call Alex Appliance Repair.
