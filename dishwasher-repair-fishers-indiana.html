Dishwasher Issues We Resolve in Fishers

  • Not draining: Water sitting in the bottom of the tub after a cycle indicates a blocked drain filter, kinked hose, or failed drain pump. We clear blockages and replace failed components.
  • Dishes not getting clean: Clogged spray arm holes, a dirty filter, failing wash motor, or low water temperature all result in poor cleaning performance. We test and restore full washing function.
  • Dishwasher leaking: Water pooling under or around the appliance suggests a damaged door gasket, loose hose fitting, or cracked sump. Leaks that reach hardwood floors or subfloor cause expensive damage - address them promptly.
  • Won't start: A faulty door latch assembly, blown thermal fuse, or control board failure can prevent the dishwasher from initiating a cycle.
  • Dishes still wet after drying cycle: A failed heating element or broken rinse aid dispenser results in dishes that need towel-drying. We restore the drying function properly.
  • Dishwasher making loud noise: Grinding or humming during the wash cycle usually points to a worn wash pump motor or debris inside the pump. We inspect and clear or replace as needed.
  • Bad odor from the dishwasher: Trapped food debris in the filter, door gasket, or drain can produce persistent odors. We clean and inspect all drain components.
  • Control panel errors or unresponsive buttons: Electronic control boards in modern Bosch and Samsung dishwashers can fail. We test the board and related components and replace what's needed.
