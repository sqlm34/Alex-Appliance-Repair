FAQ

Appliance repair FAQ

Find answers to common questions about fixing dishwashers, washing machines, refrigerators, and more, ensuring your appliances work efficiently

01. How do I know if my appliance needs repair or replacement?

You`ll know if your appliance needs repair or replacement based on age, repair cost, efficiency issues, and consistent malfunctioning signs.

02. What are common signs that my refrigerator needs servicing?

Common signs include unusual noises, inconsistent cooling, excessive frost buildup, water leaks, or increased energy consumption. These issues indicate your refrigerator might need servicing soon.

03. Why is my washing machine making unusual noises?

Unusual noises in your washing machine could indicate issues with the drum, motor, or bearings. It’s best to schedule professional repair.

04. How often should I service my appliances to keep them running smoothly?

To keep your appliances running smoothly, schedule maintenance once a year. Regular servicing helps identify potential issues early and extends the lifespan of appliances.
