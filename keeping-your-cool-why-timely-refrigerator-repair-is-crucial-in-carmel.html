Maintaining Your Refrigerator to Prevent Future Issues  

While professional repair is sometimes necessary, there are steps you can take to maintain your refrigerator and potentially prevent future problems:  

Clean the Condenser Coils Regularly: Dust and debris buildup on the coils can reduce efficiency and lead to overheating.  

Check and Clean Door Seals: Ensure the door seals are clean and create a tight seal to prevent cold air from escaping.  

Don't Overload the Refrigerator: Overpacking can restrict airflow and reduce cooling efficiency.  

Defrost Regularly (for manual defrost models): Excessive frost buildup can hinder performance.  

Ensure Proper Ventilation: Make sure there is adequate space around the refrigerator for proper airflow.
