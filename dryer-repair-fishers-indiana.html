Dryer Problems We Fix in Fishers

  • No heat (gas dryer): A failed igniter, open thermal fuse, or faulty gas valve solenoid are the most common causes. We carry igniter assemblies and gas valve kits for all major brands.
  • No heat (electric dryer): A burned-out heating element or blown thermal cutoff are typical culprits. We test the complete heating circuit and replace failed components.
  • Drum won't tumble: A broken drive belt is the most common cause. We also inspect the idler pulley and drum support rollers, which wear out and allow the drum to stop rotating.
  • Takes too long to dry clothes: Restricted exhaust airflow is the number-one cause. We check the lint filter, exhaust duct, and external vent cap, and clean or repair as needed.
  • Dryer overheats: A faulty cycling thermostat or blocked exhaust vent causes dangerous overheating. We resolve both the symptom and the underlying cause.
  • Dryer stops mid-cycle: A failing motor thermal overload protector or defective control board can cause unexpected shutdowns.
  • Loud squealing or rumbling: Worn drum felt seals, damaged drum glides, or failing drum bearings create noise that worsens over time. We inspect and replace all worn internal components.
  • Dryer door won't stay closed: A broken door latch or damaged door spring prevents the dryer from operating. We replace the latch assembly and test door operation before leaving.

Gas Dryer Repair in Fishers, Indiana

Gas dryers require specialized handling due to the presence of combustible fuel. Our technicians are trained in gas appliance safety and carry the tools needed to test gas pressure, check valve operation, and verify complete combustion. If your gas dryer in Fishers is running but not heating, or if you smell gas near the appliance, call us right away.

Electric Dryer Repair in Fishers, Indiana

Electric dryers are common in Fishers apartments, condos, and many single-family homes. The heating system involves a high-wattage element and a series of thermostats and thermal safety devices. We test each component in the circuit to identify the specific failure and replace only what's necessary.

Dryer Vent Safety in Fishers Homes

A clogged dryer vent is the leading cause of dryer-related house fires. We inspect the full exhaust path on every dryer service call in Fishers - from the duct connection at the dryer to the exterior vent cap. If we find dangerous lint accumulation, we'll recommend a vent cleaning. Many Fishers homes with long or bent vent runs are especially prone to blockages.
