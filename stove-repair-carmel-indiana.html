Stove and Oven Problems We Fix in Carmel

  • Burner won't ignite (gas): A dirty or cracked igniter, clogged burner port, or faulty spark module can prevent a gas burner from lighting. We clean, test, and replace components as needed.
  • Oven not heating: In electric ovens, a burned-out bake or broil element is the most common cause. In gas ovens, a failed igniter or gas valve is usually responsible. Both are routine repairs we complete daily.
  • Oven heats unevenly: Uneven cooking is frustrating and often caused by a failing temperature sensor or bake element. We calibrate the oven and replace faulty sensors to restore consistent heat distribution.
  • Oven runs too hot or too cool: A miscalibrated thermostat or defective temperature sensor leads to overcooked or undercooked food. We test and recalibrate or replace the sensor.
  • Control panel not responding: Modern stoves and ovens use electronic control boards that can fail. We diagnose control panel issues and replace failed boards with compatible parts.
  • Gas smell from stove: If you smell gas near your stove, turn off the appliance, ventilate the area, and call us immediately. A faulty gas valve or damaged ignition component can cause dangerous gas leaks.
  • Oven door won't close properly: A broken door hinge, worn door gasket, or bent door frame causes heat loss and inefficient cooking. We inspect and repair the door assembly completely.
  • Self-cleaning cycle not working: A failed door lock motor or control board malfunction can prevent the self-clean function from engaging.

Gas vs Electric Stove Repair in Carmel, Indiana

Gas ranges are popular in Carmel for their precise heat control and cooking performance. When a gas stove develops problems, specialized knowledge is essential. Our technicians are trained to safely work on gas appliances, including diagnosing and replacing igniters, gas valve solenoids, flame sensors, and spark switches. If you smell gas at any point, leave the home and call your gas provider before calling us.

Electric ranges - including smooth-top and coil models - have their own set of failure patterns. Heating elements burn out, control boards develop faults, and surface element switches wear out over time. We carry replacement elements and boards for all major electric range brands used in Carmel homes.

Safety First: When to Call for Stove Repair Immediately in Carmel

Some stove issues can wait a day or two. Others require immediate action. Call us right away - or contact your gas provider first - if you experience: a persistent gas odor near the appliance, sparking or arcing from an electric element, smoke coming from behind the control panel, or any signs of electrical burning. These situations are not DIY repairs and should not be ignored.
