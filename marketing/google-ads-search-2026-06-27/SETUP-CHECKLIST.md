# Google Ads Setup Checklist

1. Open Google Ads in Chrome.
2. Confirm billing, account status, business verification, and linked Google Business Profile.
3. Tools > Goals > Conversions:
   - Create phone call conversion from ads.
   - Create website phone click conversion.
   - Create booking click conversion.
   - Create contact form submit conversion.
4. Link Google Ads to GA4 and GTM if not already linked.
5. Create shared negative keyword list from 05-negative-keywords.csv.
6. Create campaigns from 01-campaign-structure.csv.
7. Add ad groups from 02-ad-groups.csv.
8. Import keywords from 03-keywords-google-ads-editor.csv.
9. Add RSAs from 04-responsive-search-ads.csv.
10. Add call, sitelink, callout, and structured snippet assets from 06-assets.csv.
11. Keep campaigns paused until tracking test passes.
12. Test: ad preview, phone click, booking click, contact form, Google Tag Assistant.
13. Enable Carmel and Fishers first. Add the other cities after 3-5 days or once tracking is stable.
