Is This What Your GE Appliance Is Doing?

These are the most common symptoms Fishers homeowners describe when calling about their GE appliances. If any of these match your situation, call (463) 248-8429 for same-day service:

"Water dispenser produces no water or very little water"

GE water dispenser failures are usually caused by a frozen fill tube in the door, a failed water inlet valve, or a broken dispenser actuator switch. We diagnose the exact cause before replacing any part.

"Washer starts then immediately stops with locked lid"

GE top-load washers use a lid lock mechanism that frequently fails — the lid appears locked but the cycle won't advance. Lid lock replacement is a quick same-day repair.

"Oven temperature is consistently off"

GE ovens with temperature calibration issues almost always need a new temperature sensor (RTD probe). This is a 30-minute repair that restores accurate baking and roasting performance.
