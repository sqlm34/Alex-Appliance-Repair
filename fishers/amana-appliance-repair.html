Is This What Your Amana Appliance Is Doing?

These are the most common symptoms Fishers homeowners describe when calling about their Amana appliances. If any of these match your situation, call (463) 248-8429 for same-day service:

"Refrigerator section is warm but freezer is fine"

This symptom on Amana refrigerators almost always points to a failed evaporator fan motor. The freezer maintains temperature because the evaporator coils are there, but the fan isn't pushing cold air into the fresh food section.

"Washer spins slowly or won't reach full spin speed"

Amana top-load washer spin problems are often caused by a worn motor coupling — a plastic and rubber coupler between the motor and transmission that absorbs shock but wears out over time. This is an inexpensive repair.

"Oven won't heat but stove burners work normally"

Amana oven bake element failure is easy to diagnose visually — look for a cracked or burnt spot on the element loop at the bottom of the oven cavity. We carry replacement elements for many current Amana models.
