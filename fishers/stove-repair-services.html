Common Stove Problems We Repair

Here are some of the most frequent problems we solve with our professional stove repair services:

  • Stove Not Turning On - If your stove won't power up, it may be caused by:
    • A damaged outlet or electrical connection
    • A malfunctioning control board
    • Gas flow issues in gas-powered models
    Our technicians will quickly identify the source and safely restore power to your stove.

  • Burners Not Heating or Heating Unevenly - When burners don't heat up properly or distribute heat unevenly, it's usually due to:
    • Burnt-out heating elements in electric stoves
    • Blocked or dirty burners in gas stoves
    We'll assess the situation and clean, repair, or replace parts as necessary for consistent performance.

  • Oven Not Heating Correctly - If your oven struggles to reach or maintain temperature, the cause might be:
    • A worn-out thermostat
    • A defective heating element
    • A malfunctioning mode selector switch
    Our team will conduct a thorough diagnostic and make the right repairs so you can enjoy evenly cooked meals again.

  • Control Panel or Display Malfunctions - Problems like flickering displays or unresponsive buttons often point to:
    • Electrical shorts or damaged circuitry
    • Faulty sensors
    • Software glitches
    We'll reset, repair, or replace the control components to get your stove back in working order.

  • Sparks or Unusual Burning Smell - This issue is serious and requires immediate attention. Common causes include:
    • Frayed or exposed wiring
    • Overheating components
    • Electrical failure
    If you notice sparks or a burning odor, shut off your stove immediately and contact our team for urgent service.
