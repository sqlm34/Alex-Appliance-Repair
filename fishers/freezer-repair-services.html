Preventive Measures to Extend Your Freezer's Lifespan

By following these simple steps, you can ensure your appliance runs efficiently and avoid costly repairs in the future.

  • Clean the Coils Regularly
    Clean the coils at least once every six months using a coil brush or a vacuum with a hose attachment.
  • Check the Door Seals
    Inspect the door seals regularly for cracks, tears, or any signs of wear. If the seals aren’t making a proper seal, replace them immediately to ensure energy efficiency and proper cooling.
  • Defrost the Freezer
    If you notice frost building up, it’s time to defrost your freezer. Manual defrost models require this to be done regularly (every few months).
  • Keep the Freezer at the Right Temperature
    Use a thermometer to check the temperature inside your freezer regularly. Adjust the settings to maintain an optimal 0°F (-18°C).
