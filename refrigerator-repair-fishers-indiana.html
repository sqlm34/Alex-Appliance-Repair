Refrigerator Problems We Fix in Fishers

  • Refrigerator not cooling: The most urgent problem. Could be a failed compressor, dirty condenser coils, malfunctioning evaporator fan, or refrigerant leak. We identify the exact cause and provide an honest recommendation.
  • Refrigerator freezing food: A thermostat failure or damper control issue can cause the fridge compartment to freeze food. We test and replace temperature regulation components.
  • Water leaking inside or under the unit: A clogged defrost drain, damaged water line, or faulty ice maker fill valve are common culprits. We trace the leak and stop it at the source.
  • Ice maker not making ice: Fishers homes with ice maker-equipped refrigerators frequently need ice maker arm, water inlet valve, or ice mold thermostat repairs.
  • Refrigerator running constantly: Continuous operation without cycling off means the compressor is overworked - usually due to a refrigerant issue, dirty coils, or door seal failure.
  • Noisy refrigerator: Clicking, buzzing, rattling, or loud humming can indicate a failing compressor relay, worn condenser fan motor, or ice maker cycling issues.
  • Door not sealing properly: A worn or torn door gasket allows warm air in and cold air out. Replacing the gasket restores efficiency and protects the compressor from overwork.
  • Water dispenser not working: A frozen water line, failed inlet valve, or clogged filter can stop the dispenser from working. We diagnose and restore full dispenser function.

Refrigerator Types We Repair in Fishers, Indiana

  • French door refrigerators: Popular in newer Fishers homes. Complex models with bottom freezer drawers and dual evaporator systems.
  • Side-by-side refrigerators: Common in Fishers homes built in the 2000s and 2010s. Frequently need door gasket, dispenser, and ice maker repairs.
  • Top freezer and bottom freezer models: The most reliable and cost-effective to repair. We carry parts for all standard configurations.
  • Counter-depth and built-in refrigerators: High-end models integrated into Fishers kitchen cabinetry. We service Sub-Zero, Thermador, and other luxury brands.
  • Smart refrigerators: LG ThinQ, Samsung Family Hub, and similar connected models. We service both the mechanical and electronic systems.
