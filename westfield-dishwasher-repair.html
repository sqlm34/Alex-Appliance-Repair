Signs Your Dishwasher Needs Repair

A well-functioning dishwasher saves time and effort, but when problems arise, it's best to address them early. If you notice any of the following issues, it's time to call for professional dishwasher repair services:

  • Dishes Come Out Dirty - If your dishwasher isn't cleaning properly, it could be due to a failing spray arm, clogged filter, or weak water pressure.
  • Water Leaks Around the Dishwasher - A leaking dishwasher may indicate a worn-out door seal, loose connections, or a damaged inlet valve.
  • Unusual Noises During Operation - Grinding, buzzing, or rattling sounds can signal motor issues, worn bearings, or debris stuck in the pump.
  • Dishwasher Won't Start - If your dishwasher fails to power on, it may be caused by a faulty door latch, control panel issues, or electrical problems.
  • Foul Odors After a Cycle - Persistent bad smells often result from trapped food particles, mold buildup, or drainage issues.

Preventive Maintenance Tips for a Long-Lasting Dishwasher

Regular maintenance can keep your dishwasher running efficiently and help you avoid costly repairs.


Here are some essential tips to extend the life of your appliance and reduce the need for dishwasher repair services:

  • Clean the Filter Regularly - A clogged filter can affect cleaning performance and cause drainage issues. Remove and rinse it under warm water to prevent buildup.
  • Check and Clean Spray Arms - Food particles and mineral deposits can block the spray arms, leading to poor water flow. Clean them monthly for optimal performance.
  • Use the Right Detergent - Always use dishwasher-safe detergent to prevent excessive suds and residue that could lead to malfunctions.
  • Inspect Door Seals for Leaks - A worn-out or dirty seal can cause leaks. Wipe it down regularly and replace it if you notice cracks or damage.
  • Run Hot Water Before Starting a Cycle - Letting hot water flow through your sink before running the dishwasher improves cleaning efficiency.
  • Schedule Professional Maintenance - Even with proper care, professional servicing can help detect issues early. If you need expert help, contact us for dishwasher repair in Westfield, Indiana to keep your appliance in top condition.

Dishwasher repair FAQ

Find answers to common questions about Westfield Dishwasher repair, and more, ensuring your appliances work efficiently

01. What should I avoid putting in my dishwasher to prevent damage?

Avoid washing wooden utensils, cast iron, non-stick pans, and delicate glassware, as they can get damaged or cause blockages.

02. Can I fix my dishwasher myself instead of calling a professional?

While simple issues like clogged filters can be handled at home, electrical and mechanical problems require professional dishwasher repair services.

03. Why is my dishwasher overfilling with water?

A faulty water inlet valve or float switch can cause overfilling. Our technicians can quickly resolve this issue.

04. What should I do if my dishwasher trips the circuit breaker?

This may indicate an electrical issue or faulty wiring. Turn off the appliance and call us for a professional repair.

05. Is it better to repair or replace an old dishwasher?

If your dishwasher is over 10 years old and has frequent issues, replacement may be more cost-effective. However, we can assess and provide the best recommendation.

06. Can a dishwasher be repaired if it has rust inside?

Yes, but extensive rust may indicate that a replacement is a better option. We can inspect and advise accordingly.

07. Do you offer emergency dishwasher repair?

Yes, we provide emergency dishwasher repair services for urgent issues like leaks and electrical failures.
